What Drives Us

At KrosLinkFinanceOnlineBanking, our journey over the past 90 years has been guided by values of honour, enterprise, unity, and commitment.

As a trusted name in digital banking, we are committed to shaping a smarter financial future for individuals and businesses across regions.

With a seamless online platform and a strong regional network, we deliver modern, secure, and accessible financial solutions to help our customers thrive.

Everything we do is driven by our promise to do what’s right — for our customers, our colleagues, and the wider community we proudly serve.

Doing Right by You – 90 Years and Beyond with KrosLinkFinanceOnlineBanking

$30 Million in Education Support

KrosLinkFinanceOnlineBanking empowers over 120,000 children and students from underprivileged backgrounds through purpose-driven education programmes, creating lasting financial opportunity.

$110 Million to Strengthen Education

In collaboration with the government, KrosLinkFinanceOnlineBanking and the Wee Foundation are contributing to a $275 million endowment to expand access and open doors for students nationwide.

20,000 Children Gain Digital Access

Through our UOB My Digital Space initiative, KrosLinkFinanceOnlineBanking equips children from disadvantaged families with digital tools and skills vital for a thriving future in the digital economy.

90 Days of Pocket Money

KrosLinkFinanceOnlineBanking supports 11,000 students from low-income families by providing pocket money for 90 school days — helping ease the financial burden and keep education uninterrupted.
