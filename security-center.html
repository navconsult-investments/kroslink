Security Center

Your trust is our top priority — Learn how we protect your financial data and how you can stay secure.

1. How We Secure Your Information

At KrosLinkFinanceOnlineBanking, the security of your personal and financial data is our highest concern. We use industry-standard encryption protocols (TLS 1.3), advanced firewalls, and continuous monitoring systems to guard your data against unauthorized access. Every transaction is authenticated and monitored in real time to prevent fraud and ensure transparency.

2. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

We require multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all logins and sensitive transactions. This extra layer of security ensures that even if someone gets your password, they won’t gain access to your account. MFA includes one-time passcodes (OTPs), biometric verification, and device-based trust scoring.

3. Fraud Detection & Alerts

Our intelligent fraud detection engine uses machine learning to identify suspicious behavior instantly. If an unusual login, transfer, or activity is detected, you'll receive real-time alerts via SMS, email, and push notifications. You can temporarily freeze your account via the app until the situation is verified.

4. Data Privacy & Compliance

We are fully compliant with global data privacy laws such as the GDPR, NDPR, and PCI DSS. We never sell your data or share it with unauthorized third parties. Your financial footprint stays secure within our encrypted infrastructure and is only accessible by you and trusted KrosLinkFinance personnel.

5. Secure Mobile & Web Banking

Our mobile app and web platforms are regularly penetration-tested and certified for high-level security standards. All communications between your device and our servers are encrypted using state-of-the-art technologies, and the app is built to prevent data leakage and malware interference.

6. Your Role in Staying Safe

Security is a partnership. We advise all customers to use strong, unique passwords, avoid public Wi-Fi for banking, and report any suspicious activity immediately. Never share your PIN, OTP, or password with anyone. If in doubt, always reach out to our 24/7 support line before taking action.

7. What to Do If You Suspect Fraud

If you believe your account has been compromised or if you’ve noticed unauthorized transactions, contact us immediately via support@kroslinkfinancial.com or call our fraud hotline. You can also lock your account temporarily through the banking app for additional safety while we investigate.
