Latest Updates

Company Updates - 2025

1. Expansion into New Markets:
KrosLinkFinanceOnlineBanking is proud to announce its expansion into three new countries across Africa and Asia. This strategic growth reflects our commitment to increasing financial inclusion and delivering top-tier digital banking services globally.

2. Mobile App Upgrade:
Our newly updated mobile app introduces biometric login, 24/7 virtual assistant support, and real-time loan application tracking. We’re committed to providing secure, intuitive, and customer-first banking experiences.

3. Sustainability Initiatives:
We’ve launched a new green finance program to support eco-conscious entrepreneurs and sustainable development projects. KrosLink is investing $50 million into climate-friendly loan schemes by 2026.

4. Cybersecurity Reinforcement:
With the rise of cyber threats, our IT division has implemented state-of-the-art AI-based fraud detection systems. Customers can now enjoy enhanced data protection across all platforms.

5. Financial Education Portal:
We recently launched the KrosLink Financial Literacy Hub – a free, open-access portal with resources on budgeting, investments, credit management, and loan planning.

6. New Loan Products:
Two new products have been introduced: the Emergency Relief Loan (ERL) and Youth Empowerment Loan (YEL), both aimed at providing instant support for underbanked populations.

7. Community Engagement:
Our annual “KrosLink Impact Week” helped over 10,000 individuals with free banking seminars, job skills training, and small business funding clinics in rural areas.
